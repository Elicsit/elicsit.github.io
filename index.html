Who are we

Once upon a time, our journey began as a mere joke. Blame it on my aunt, the eternal sidetrack sensation – give her a simple task like making coffee, and she'll surprise you with a full-fledged bloekom tree plantation. Ever been there? We bet you have, or at least someone you know has pulled a similar stunt.

But hey, before you wander off on your own mental tangent, let us introduce ourselves. We're a bunch of laid-back youngsters who stumbled upon these effortlessly cool designs and thought, "Why not turn this into a business?" Nestled in the sunny vibes of Port Alfred, South Africa, we birthed a chic, minimalistic, retro-inspired brand: Side Track Susan.

So, whether you're here intentionally or got sidetracked like the best of us, grab a shirt, spread the good vibes, and pick your favorite design. Meanwhile, I'll try to get back to making my coffee – emphasis on "try." Cheers to the chill side of life!
